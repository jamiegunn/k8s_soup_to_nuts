## Why this site exists

Most Kubernetes material assumes you administer the cluster. In many
organizations you don't: a platform team owns the nodes, the CNI, the
ingress controllers, and the RBAC — and you own everything that happens
inside your namespace. This guide is written for that reality. Every article
assumes namespace-level access, `kubectl exec` into your own pods, and a
paved-road CI/CD pipeline you must not fight.
